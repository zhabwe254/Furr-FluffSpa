About Fur & Fluff E-Spa

The Fur & Fluff E-Spa project was inspired by the need to simplify and enhance the pet grooming experience. By providing an online platform for appointment booking, customer management, and inventory tracking, we aim to make pet grooming more efficient and enjoyable for both pet owners and service providers.

This project was developed as a part of the Portfolio Project for Holberton School.
